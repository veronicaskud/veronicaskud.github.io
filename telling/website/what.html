We help educators create more cinematic lectures that are experienced and discovered.

Telling Story products and Creative Services enable educators to deliver Flipped Instruction using our innovative approach—Documentary Instruction:

  • Discrete Episodes—keep students engaged.
  • Synchronized Content—supports and enriches the narrative.
  • Stop Questions & Discussion—drive participation.
  • Responsive design—allows learners to follow the story from desktop to laptop, tablet, and phone.

Watch, explore, interact and contribute — Engaging mobile instruction for today's students.

Instruction Produced

We’ve all had priceless moments of insight provoked by a masterful storyteller. We’ve had these experiences live, but also when watching documentary films—a medium often under-appreciated for its instructional power. We bring the talent and technology to help educators harness this power, and distribute new digital narratives in a cinematic format optimized for playback on mobile devices.

The Art of Engagement

Too much of the instructional content provided to learners today fails to inspire. By remixing instructional delivery with popular consumer technology formats, we tilt the odds in favor of educators vying for attention. The result is highly produced, more convenient for concept review, and more captivating as a performance. We believe these are meaningful contributions to improve student engagement.

Technology

Our finished works integrate seamlessly with any modern LMS compliant with open standards for learning tool interoperability. We supplement LMS platforms with film-industry grade cloud video distribution to ensure scaled playback by learners.

Guided by Analytics

One of the biggest challenges in facilitating a Flipped Classroom is when students don’t come to class prepared. We understand the importance of tracking what students have seen and done and how they are progressing through content and concepts.

Creative Services

IN THE TELLING producers and course designers consult with instructors to plan and develop their course productions. We film, edit, animate and integrate instruction with minimal disruption to faculty workload.
